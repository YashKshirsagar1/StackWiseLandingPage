Privacy Policy.

Last updated: May 13, 2025

The short version: StackWise is a free educational platform. We collect only what's necessary to make the app work. We never sell your personal information. We never store your photos. All behavioral data we use for research is fully anonymized — it cannot be traced back to you.


01

Who We Are

StackWise ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered peptide intelligence platform operated by StackWise Inc. Our app is available at stackwse.com and on mobile platforms. We help users research peptides, build personalized stacks, track protocols, and log outcomes — all for free.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at: privacy@stackwse.com

02

Information We Collect

We collect only what's necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — email address and password when you create an account
  • Profile data — age range (not exact age), biological sex, health goals, experience level, and preferred administration routes. You provide this to get personalized recommendations
  • Protocol data — peptide compounds you log, dosages, cycle durations, and administration routes
  • Outcome data — self-reported check-ins at weeks 4, 8, 12, and 16 of active cycles, including wellbeing scores and side effect reports
  • Journal entries — text entries you write. These are processed for sentiment and topics, then the raw text is discarded. We never store raw journal content in our dataset
  • AI chatbot interactions — query categories and peptide references extracted from your questions. We never store the raw text of your questions
  • Usage data — standard app usage information such as which features you use, session duration, and app performance data

03

Information We Do NOT Collect

We are explicit about what we never collect:

  • Photos — Self Scan photos are processed immediately on-device or in memory and permanently discarded. No photo is ever stored in our systems under any circumstances
  • Exact age — we collect age ranges only (e.g. 25-34), never your date of birth
  • Exact location — we collect country only, never city, address, or precise location
  • Raw chat text — your exact chatbot questions are never stored. Only structured categories and compound references are retained
  • Raw journal text — processed into sentiment scores and topic categories only. Raw text is discarded immediately after processing
  • Payment information — StackWise is completely free. We have no payment system and collect no financial data

04

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized AI recommendations based on your goals and logged protocols
  • Power the Self Scan feature — analyze submitted photos and return recommendations in real time
  • Generate your weekly progress summaries and outcome check-in prompts
  • Improve the accuracy of our AI models and peptide-specific training
  • Build anonymized aggregate datasets for research and pharma partnerships (see Section 6)
  • Send app notifications and optional email communications (you can opt out at any time)
  • Ensure the security and performance of our platform

05

Data Anonymization

Your personal identity and your behavioral data are separated at the database architecture level. Here is exactly how:

  • Your account (email, password) lives in our authentication system only
  • Your behavioral data (protocols, outcomes, journal sentiment) is stored under a randomly generated UUID — a string of letters and numbers with no connection to your name or email
  • There is a hard technical wall between authentication data and behavioral data. They are never joined or linked in our dataset tables
  • Age ranges, country, and biological sex are the only demographic data points in the dataset — and none of these alone or combined can identify you

What this means in practice: Even StackWise employees cannot look at a behavioral record and know whose it is. The anonymization is architectural, not just policy-based.

06

Pharma Research and Data Licensing

StackWise's long-term business model involves licensing anonymized aggregate behavioral data to pharmaceutical research companies and academic institutions. This is how we sustain a completely free product.

Here is exactly what this means for you:

  • We may share aggregate, anonymized insights — for example: "Users who ran BPC-157 at 250mcg for 8 weeks reported a mean outcome score of 7.8/10." No individual is identifiable in this data
  • We never sell individual records or any data that could be traced to a specific person
  • We never share email addresses, names, or account information with any third party for any purpose
  • All data shared with research partners has passed through our anonymization architecture described in Section 5
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 9). Upon deletion, your UUID is removed from all dataset tables and cannot be reconstructed

07

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate StackWise:

  • Supabase — database and authentication infrastructure. Data is stored in encrypted databases with role-based access controls
  • Grok API (xAI) — powers our AI chatbot and Self Scan analysis. Photo data is transmitted to Grok for processing and immediately discarded — Grok does not retain your photos
  • Vercel — website and API hosting
  • Loops — email communications (only if you sign up for updates)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — app distribution

Each of these providers is bound by their own privacy policies and our data processing agreements. We do not authorize any third party to use your data for their own purposes.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold associated with your account
  • Deletion — request complete deletion of your account and all associated data from our systems, including the anonymized behavioral dataset
  • Correction — update or correct any profile information at any time within the app settings
  • Opt out of communications — unsubscribe from any email communications at any time
  • Data portability — request an export of your personal data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@stackwse.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10

Children's Privacy

StackWise is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user under 18 has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us at privacy@stackwse.com.

11

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS and at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Database access is restricted to authorized personnel with role-based permissions
  • Authentication data and behavioral data are stored in separate, isolated systems
  • We conduct regular security reviews of our infrastructure and third-party integrations

No system is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at privacy@stackwse.com.
